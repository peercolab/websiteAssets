Developing the next generation of techies

Peer Colab is a community of engineers, designers, product managers looking to grow in our separate fields. We help each other grow by learning and building together.

Our Mission

To grow juniors/beginners to mid-level techies (engineers, prod. managers and designers) by providing them with real-world projects and/or experience.

About Us

We are a non-profit focused on developing tech talent through a simulated work environment.
